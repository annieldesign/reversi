Taking the aquariums around the world as an inspiration, the Reversi fish tank is using the basic Reversi rule, where player with the fish avatar gets to move first. On your turn, you place one piece on the board with your sea creature facing up. You must place the piece so that an opponent's piece, or a row of opponent's pieces, is flanked by your pieces. Play against the computer, or challenge your friends.
